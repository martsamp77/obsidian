## Notes

**Apple ID warning:** Apple ties billing email to your Apple ID. Changing it is a bigger operation than a standard account email update — verify whether you want to change the Apple ID itself or just the billing contact before proceeding.

**Synchrony Bank covers multiple cards:** One account update covers Verizon Visa Signature, PayPal Credit, Sam's Club World Elite Mastercard, and Venmo Credit Card. Confirm all four cards are under one Synchrony login before updating.

**Retreat at Greystone uses three systems:** RentCafe, Entrata, and Flex are separate platforms. You may need to update the email on each one individually — check your portal logins for each.

**Stripe follows Cursor:** You don't need to contact Stripe directly. Update the email on your Cursor account and Stripe receipts will automatically go to the new address.
