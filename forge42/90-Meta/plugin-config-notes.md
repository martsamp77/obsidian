- **Templates** (core): `.obsidian/templates.json` — folder `02-Templates`.
- **Templater** (community): Recommended in [[Monday and Obsidian Organization]]; needed for `{{date:YYYY-MM-DD}}` and similar in `daily-note.md` unless you switch to core `{{date}}` placeholders.
