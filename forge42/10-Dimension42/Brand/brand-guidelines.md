# Dimension42 Brand Guidelines

Visual identity reference for the Dimension42 tech brand. Use this when creating any D42-branded assets, graphics, or content.

## Brand Identity

Dimension42 sits at the intersection of physics, AI, and the number 42 (Hitchhiker's Guide reference). The brand should feel: **technical, slightly sci-fi, intelligent, premium.**

It should NOT feel: playful, cartoonish, overly corporate, or like a startup.

Think: the logo you'd see on a black terminal screen or embossed on machined aluminum.

## Color Palette

| Role | Color | Hex |
|---|---|---|
| Primary background | Deep navy | `#0A1628` |
| Accent | Electric cyan | `#00D4FF` |
| Text on dark | Clean white | `#FFFFFF` |
| Secondary text | Cool gray | `#8892A0` |
| Alternative navy | Darker navy | `#0D1B2A` |
| Alternative cyan | Softer cyan | `#00B4D8` |

**Usage:**
- Navy is the primary background for all D42 content
- Cyan is the single accent color — use sparingly and with intention
- White and gray for body text and secondary elements
- Never use warm colors (orange, amber, red) on D42 assets — those belong to Forge42/Marty

## Typography

*Logo generation tools will establish the final typeface. Guidelines:*

- **Style:** Clean modern sans-serif — geometric, angular, no serifs
- **Weight:** Medium to bold for headings; regular for body
- **Character:** Technical precision, not playfulness
- **Avoid:** Script fonts, slab serifs, overly rounded letterforms

## Logo Concepts

D42 has three logo elements to generate and finalize:
1. **Wordmark** — "Dimension42" as a typographic unit, with "42" integrated
2. **Brand mark / icon** — standalone icon (tesseract, portal, dimensional geometry) that works at 32px favicon size
3. **Monogram** — "D42" fused into a single symbol

For generation prompts using Nano Banana or similar AI image tools, see [[brand-graphics]].

**Required logo variants per final design:**
- Full color on white background
- Full color on dark (navy) background
- Single color (white) version
- Single color (black) version
- Icon only (no text)
- Text only (no icon)

## Voice and Tone

| Attribute | Yes | No |
|---|---|---|
| Technical depth | Yes — D42 speaks to builders | No jargon without explanation |
| Confident | Yes — authoritative, not arrogant | No hype, no buzzword stacking |
| Sci-fi edge | Subtle references to physics/space/dimensions | Not literal aliens or space imagery |
| Personal | Never — D42 is a brand, not a person | The zero-human story lives on Marty's brand, not D42's |

## D42 vs. Marty Sampson Brand

| Dimension42 | Marty Sampson |
|---|---|
| Products and tech | The story of building those products |
| Faceless — AI-produced content only | Hybrid — face appears on camera |
| Never mentions "zero employees" | Zero-human narrative lives here |
| Delivers value | Tells the story |

## Content Aesthetic

**YouTube thumbnails:** Dark navy background, bold white headline, cyan accent words, 1280×720px

**Social graphics:** Dark background, minimal layout, single accent color, no gradients or 3D effects

**Blog/web:** Dark theme preferred, code blocks as prominent visual element

## Relationship to Other Brands

- **37Metrics** — legal owner; appears in footers and legal pages only
- **Forge42** — internal infrastructure; never appears in D42 customer-facing content
- **Eddie** — internal agent; may appear as a feature of D42 products but not as a customer-facing brand character
