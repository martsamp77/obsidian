# 37Metrics — Mission & Purpose

## The Mission

**To build AI-native software products and media properties that generate compounding revenue — operated by one person and AI agents, with zero employees.**

## What 37Metrics Is

37Metrics, LLC is the legal and operational foundation that makes the mission possible. It is not a brand. It is not customer-facing. It is the organizational infrastructure that holds everything together.

- It owns the DBA for Dimension42
- It signs contracts and invoices
- It files taxes
- It provides liability protection
- It is the entity that becomes Marty Sampson, Inc. — even if Marty is the only human in the room

## What It Enables

| Entity | What 37Metrics makes possible |
|---|---|
| **Dimension42** | Can operate as a public tech brand, ship products, and earn revenue under LLC protection |
| **Marty Sampson** | Can consult, speak, and contract through a professional legal entity |
| **Both** | Clean IP ownership, clear tax structure, and a credible foundation for future investment or acquisition |

## The Guiding Principle

37Metrics succeeds when it is **invisible**. If clients, partners, or the public ever have to think about 37Metrics, something went wrong. It belongs in footers, on invoices, and in the accountant's files. The brands it enables — Dimension42 and Marty Sampson — belong everywhere else.

## The Bigger Picture

The real mission isn't legal administration. It's proving that one person, operating with AI agents as a full workforce, can build a software company, a media company, and a consulting practice simultaneously — and make them all profitable.

37Metrics is the legal wrapper around that experiment. Dimension42 is where the products ship. Marty Sampson is where the story gets told.

## How the Mission Gets Executed

The operating model that makes this mission real: [[operating-model]] — the Two-Suite Architecture.

37Metrics runs the Business Planning Suite (strategy, product definition, market research). Dimension42 receives the output (shipped products). Forge42 runs the infrastructure. Marty directs it all from 7 defined checkpoints.
