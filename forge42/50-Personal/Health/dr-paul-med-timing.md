## Key Spacing & Interaction Notes

- **Caffeine cutoff: 1:00 PM.** No caffeine sources after lunch — including Ultra pouches.
- **Calcium and magnesium:** Take at separate meals when possible. Calcium competes with magnesium for absorption.
- **Vitamin D3 + K2:** Always take together, with a fat-containing meal. K2 directs calcium to bones and away from arteries.
- **Potassium + Olmesartan:** ARBs promote potassium retention. The HCTZ component partially offsets this. Monitor serum K at labs.
- **Creatine:** Timing doesn't matter much — consistency is what matters. Morning anchors the habit.
- **Methylene Blue:** Contraindicated with SSRIs, SNRIs, MAOIs (serotonin syndrome risk). Not currently on any of these.
