Per the channel strategy — produce these first, before launching:

2. **Second video:** Behind-the-scenes of the AI stack (AI-produced)
3. **Third video:** Tool comparison or tutorial (AI-produced)
4. Launch with these 3 videos in the first week
5. Build cadence before worrying about Shorts
