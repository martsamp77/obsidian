# YouTube Strategy: Marty Sampson

Hybrid personal brand channel. This is where the zero-human narrative lives on video.

## Channel Identity

- **Handle:** @martysampson (or closest available)
- **Format:** 70% AI-produced, 30% on camera
- **Face appears in:** Intros, commentary segments, opinion pieces, behind-the-scenes of building the zero-human company
- **AI-produced content:** Tutorials, explainers, news roundups, deep dives into AI tools

## The Core Narrative

> "I'm one person building a software company with zero employees using AI. Here's how."

YouTube's 2026 algorithm looks for a "human creative fingerprint." On-camera segments protect AI-produced content from authenticity flags and win sponsorship deals because advertisers see a human anchor.

## Content Pillars

- Zero-human company building (the main story arc)
- AI tool reviews and comparisons
- Personal journey / progress updates (revenue, failures, milestones)
- Technical tutorials (hosting, stacks, agents) — accessible, not deeply technical

## Posting Cadence

- Long-form: 1–2 per week
- Shorts: 3–5 per week (repurposed from long-form clips)

## Cross-Posting

- Shorts → TikTok, Instagram Reels, X
- Channel naturally references D42: "I built this using my Dimension42 platform..."
- Never the reverse — D42 channel is standalone

## Launch Sequence

1. First video: "I'm building a company with zero employees — here's why and how"
2. Follow immediately with a behind-the-scenes video of the AI stack
3. Establish a posting rhythm before worrying about Shorts
4. D42 channel launches only after Marty channel has a stable content pipeline
