# dimension42.ai Website

## What Is It

The public-facing website for the Dimension42 tech brand. Serves as the content hub, product home, blog, and documentation site for everything D42.

## Why It Matters

This is where revenue lives. Products need a home. YouTube viewers need somewhere to land. The website establishes the brand as a real company with real products. Build priority is high.

## Which Brand

- [x] Dimension42 — this IS the D42 brand online presence

## Rough Scope

Medium (weeks for MVP, ongoing iteration) — launch a solid landing page + blog fast, then add product pages as products ship.

## Target Pages

- `/` — Hero: AI-native software company, email signup, links to YouTube and products
- `/blog` — Technical blog, tutorials, AI tool coverage
- `/products` — Product pages as they launch
- `/docs` — Documentation for products
- `/about` — Company story, the D42/37Metrics relationship
- `/support` — Support contact
