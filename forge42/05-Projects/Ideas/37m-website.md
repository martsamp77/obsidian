# 37metrics.com Website

## What Is It

The corporate identity page for 37Metrics, LLC. Simple, professional, minimal. Establishes the holding company's online presence for clients, partners, accountants, and lawyers.

## Why It Matters

When someone receives an invoice from 37Metrics, LLC and Googles the company name, they should find a clean, professional page that explains what the entity is and links to the brands it operates. A blank domain or a parked page is unprofessional.

## Which Brand

- [x] 37Metrics — legal entity page

## Rough Scope

Small (days) — one page with minimal content. This is not a content site.

## Target Content

- LLC name, brief description ("holding company for Dimension42 and Marty Sampson")
- Links to: dimension42.ai, martysampson.com
- Legal: /privacy, /terms (for software product ToS)
- Contact: link to 37Metrics Office 365 email for business inquiries

## Notes

- Email stays on Office 365 for 37metrics.com — do NOT move to FastMail
- Build priority: low. A one-page HTML site is sufficient for now.
- This site does NOT need a blog, social presence, or content strategy
