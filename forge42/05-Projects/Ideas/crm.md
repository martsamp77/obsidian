# Custom CRM

## What Is It

A custom-built CRM to manage client relationships, consulting contacts, and business development across all three brands (37Metrics, Dimension42, Marty Sampson). Replaces or supplements generic CRM tools with something tailored to the zero-human, AI-agent-driven model.

## Why It Matters

Off-the-shelf CRMs are built for sales teams. This operation is one person with AI agents. A custom CRM can be built to surface exactly what matters — active consulting clients, pipeline leads, YouTube collaborators, speaking contacts — without the noise of enterprise CRM bloat. It can integrate with the AI stack and FastMail architecture.

## Which Brand

- [x] 37Metrics — spans all brands, lives at the holding company level

## Rough Scope

Large (months) — this is a real application with a database, UI, and integrations.

## Notes

- Could be built as a lightweight web app or an Obsidian-native solution using Bases
- Integration targets: FastMail (contact sync), Obsidian vault (link to contact notes), possibly a simple web UI
- Potential product angle: if the CRM is good enough, it could become a D42 product for other solo operators
- Related idea: the Apple TV dashboard could pull from this CRM's data
